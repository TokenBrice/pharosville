# Code Of Conduct

## Our Standard

PharosVille contributors are expected to keep discussion technical, respectful, and useful. Good participation includes:

- focusing criticism on code, docs, data, and behavior
- explaining tradeoffs clearly
- assuming public comments may be read by future contributors
- avoiding harassment, personal attacks, slurs, threats, and unwanted attention
- keeping private security details out of public threads

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and contributions that do not meet this standard. Repeated or severe behavior may result in blocked participation in the repository.

Report conduct concerns privately to the maintainer. Do not publish private personal information or sensitive security details in a public issue.
